About Us

Experience traditional Nepalese
charm at Mitho Khana

"Mitho Khana" is a Nepali restaurant that serves delicious and authentic Nepali cuisine. The restaurant's name "Mitho Khana" translates to "delicious food" in Nepali, reflecting its focus on providing customers with high-quality and tasty food. The menu features traditional Nepali dishes such as momos, dal bhat, and chow mein, as well as other popular South Asian dishes. The restaurant also has a cozy and welcoming atmosphere that makes it the perfect spot for a casual meal or a special occasion.
